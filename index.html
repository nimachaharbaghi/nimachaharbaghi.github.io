About me

I'm a Master's Thesis Researcher specializing in Digital Twin and 5G Localization at EURECOM, Sophia Antipolis, pursuing a double degree between ISEP Paris and the University of Bologna.

My research focuses on 5G network prototyping, AI-driven localization, and digital twin technologies. I enjoy bridging the gap between theoretical wireless communication and practical system implementation, working with real 5G testbeds such as OpenAirInterface and srsRAN. I am passionate about applying machine learning to solve challenging problems in next-generation wireless networks.
